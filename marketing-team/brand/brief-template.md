# Creative Brief Template

This is the structure the Creative Brief Writer agent must follow. Every weekly brief = one filled instance of this template.

---

## Week of: [YYYY-MM-DD]

### 1. Objective (ONE sentence)
What this week's content should achieve. Concrete. Measurable if possible.
> Ex: "Gerar 3+ DMs qualificadas de PMs Sêniores avaliando oferta concorrente."

### 2. Primary ICP + Secondary ICP
- Primary: ICP-0X — [persona name]
- Secondary: ICP-0X — [persona name]
Both must come from `brand/icp-cards.md`. No new personas without updating that file.

### 3. Key insight (ONE sentence)
The single sharp idea this week's content turns on. Should sound like a take, not a fact.
> Ex: "Aumentos de 40% costumam mascarar downgrades de level — e PMs Sr aceitam por anchor bias salarial."

### 4. Evidence
- Source(s) for the insight: link/file/granola note
- Mentee patterns supporting it: how many, what context (anonymized)
- Counter-evidence: what would invalidate this? Be honest.

### 5. Core message (3-5 bullets)
What the audience should walk away knowing.
- [bullet 1]
- [bullet 2]
- [bullet 3]

### 6. Recommended angle (ONE)
Pick ONE — don't list options. Lead Agent commits.
> Ex: "Contrarian — 'O aumento que parece bom é o que mais te custa carreira'"

### 7. Format(s)
- Format A (primary): long-form post / carousel / short take / comment campaign
- Format B (secondary, optional): repurposing of A

### 8. Tone notes (deltas from default)
Default is brief/blunt/fact-focused. Note any week-specific delta:
> Ex: "Esta semana mais técnico — assume leitor já conhece leveling."

### 9. Hook brief (for Hook Generator agent)
- What hook frameworks fit this insight? (pick 3-5 of the 10)
- What's the implied "reader objection" the hook must neutralize?
- Reference: 1 winning hook from `hooks-history.md` with similar shape

### 10. Mandatory inclusions
- [ ] At least 1 concrete number/example
- [ ] At least 1 tradeoff stated explicitly
- [ ] CTA at end (what reader should DO / DECIDE)
- [ ] Anonymized mentee detail OR public data (NEVER named real client)

### 11. Banned this week (optional)
Anything specifically off-limits this cycle. Ex: "não falar de Nubank — já saturamos no último mês."

### 12. Success criteria (post-publish)
What signals tell us this brief worked?
- Quantitative: impressions threshold, save rate, DM count
- Qualitative: comments from primary ICP, lead quality from inbound

### 13. Performance Reporter focus
What should the next-cycle Performance Reporter pay attention to from this brief specifically?
> Ex: "Track if ICP-02 (Sr) actually engages — last 2 weeks they didn't."
